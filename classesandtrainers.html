Classes And Trainers

Classes

Pushing Parameters

Designed to improve speed, strength, agility and general athleticism. This class will push you to new limits. Involving explosive movements, powerlifting and stamina exercises, this class is perfect for somebody who wants to improve their overall capabilities whether for sport or for day-to-day activities.


Mixed Martial Arts

This class is not exclusively for people who are preparing for a fight! The benefits of Mixed Martial Arts training seeps into other aspects of your life, including boosted confidence, enhanced discipline and a new level of focus in mindset! Never stepped in the ring before? This class is suitable for people of all levels in martial arts training, including beginners.


Watch My Weight

Do you have specific weight targets to reach within a given time period? Perhpas you're going on holiday or trying to cut weight for a competative sport? This is the perfect class for all weight loss goals. With classroom time as well as intensive exercise sessions, this class is perfect for people who want to lose weight, regardless of their target.

Trainers

Harry

Harry specialises in strength and conditioning. He has a background in powerlifting and has helped many clients reach their strength goals. His training style is perfect for amature and professional athletes who want to push their limits and have the competative edge against their opponents but is also a great asset for the beginners who wants to incoroporate athleticism and mindset into their training and personal life. Harry works with Paula, our in-house nutritionist, to help clients achieve their strength and conditioning goals.


Paula

Paula is a professional martial artist who is a veteran in many disciplines including Jiu-Jitsu, Sambo, Muay Thai and Karate. Perfect for any level of skill, Paula offers bespoke training regimens suitable for clients wishing to compete, improve their skill level or even just for general fitness. She is also a qualified nutritionist and offers diet plans as a seperate service to her training sessions.


Jim

Looking to build that dream beach body? Jim is the man for you! Having won many bodybuilding competitions, Jim can help you to gain lean muscle mass or to lose weight (or both) to achieve your ideal physique. Jim also works closely with Paula to prepared diet plans specific for his clients' needs.
